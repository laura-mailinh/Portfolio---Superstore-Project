# Portfolio---Superstore-Project
# Business Performance Analysis & Forecasting Dashboard

## Project Overview
This project analyzes business performance using the Superstore dataset, covering sales growth, customer behavior, profitability, regional performance, and future sales forecasting. The goal is to deliver actionable business insights and support strategic decision-making. 

## Key Objectives
* Understand sales growth drivers
* Analyze customer behavior and retention
* Identify profitability risks
* Evaluate regional performance
* Forecast future sales trends

## Project Structure
### Phase 1 - Sales Performance Analysis
* Total sales increased by 20.36%
* Growth driven by customer expansion (+8.62%) and higer spending (+10.8%)
* High-value customers generated 64.83% of revenue

### Phase 2 - Customer Behavior Analysis
* Repeat customer increased by 30.77%
* Repeat customer rate reached 98.49%
* Repeat customers contributed 77.68% of total revenue

### Phase 3 - Profitability Analysis
* Profit margin declined by 5.09%
* Loss-making products increased by 29.98%
* Discounts above 30% resulted in negative profit

### Phase 4 - Regional Performance
* West region led in profitability
* Central region underperformed
* High revenue does not always equal high profit

### Phase 5 - Sales Forecasting 
* Forecast accuracy: 84.13%
* Expected growth: 25.09%
* Strong seasonality observed in Q4

## Tools & Technology
* Python (Pandas, Prophet, Statsmodels)
* Tableau
* DAX
* Data Visualization
* Forecasting Models

## Business Recommendations
* Focus on repeat customer retention
* Optimize discount policy
* Improve low-margin categories
* Address regional performance gaps
* Prepare for regional demand
* Focus on high-value customers
